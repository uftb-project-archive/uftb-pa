Welcome to the central hub for all things tech at University of Frontier Technology, Bangladesh! Whether your project runs on code, sensors, or motors, this is the place to share it. We are a community of students building software, tinkering with IoT devices, and programming robots. Use this group to show off your work, find teammates for your next big idea, and get feedback from fellow builders.
